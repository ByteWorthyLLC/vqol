# Phase 4 Research: Curiosity-Led Amplification

See `.planning/research/AMPLIFICATION.md` for the full synthesis and source links.

## Research Takeaways

- Hacker News needs intellectual curiosity, not promotion. The hook is "clinical outcomes as static local-first PWA."
- Reddit requires authentic community participation and moderator-specific rules. Do not mass-post the same launch link.
- GitHub has native conversion surfaces: social preview, topics, discussions, releases, Pages.
- WebKit storage policy makes PWA install education practical, not cosmetic.
- The most shareable product features are proof artifacts: no-backend proof, one-file fork proof, demo data, QR poster, share receipt, offline challenge.
- Channel work should wait until the proof artifacts exist.
