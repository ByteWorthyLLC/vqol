# Phase 07 Context: Viral Functionality and Marketing Loop

## Trigger

Feedback: the project still needed to feel more viral for both functionality and
marketing. The direction remained intellectual curiosity over promotion.

## Problem

Phase 6 made the app more useful with Outcomes Studio and Practice Forge, but
the share path was still mostly external prose. A visitor needed a clearer way
to turn the working routes into a public post, a clinical pilot note, a fork
path, or an AI-citable description.

## Constraints

- Marketing must be generated from real routes and artifacts.
- No claims of clinical efficacy from the software wrapper.
- No account system, analytics, backend dependency, or opaque growth mechanics.
- Launch copy must preserve the distinction between VEINES-QOL/Sym instrument
  content and this MIT-licensed application shell.
- The output should help developers, clinicians, practices, and patients take
  concrete next actions.

## Existing Inputs

- `#/results?demo=1`: fake longitudinal result.
- `#/proof`: local-first and no-telemetry proof.
- `#/studio`: synthetic cohort workbench.
- `#/forge`: practice config builder.
- `#/poster`: waiting-room QR poster.
- Public repo and GitHub Pages demo.
