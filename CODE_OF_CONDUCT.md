# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and patient utility.
- Assume healthcare deployments require extra care around privacy, accessibility, and claims.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private patient, practice, or security information in public issues.
- Pressuring maintainers to add telemetry, clinical advice, or license-unsafe instrument content.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, close, or remove issues, pull requests, or comments that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from the repository.

## Reporting

Email conduct concerns to the maintainer listed in `GOVERNANCE.md`.
